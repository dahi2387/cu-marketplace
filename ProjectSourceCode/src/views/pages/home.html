Buff Market

Welcome to Buff Market - The CU Student Marketplace! A safe and easy way for students to buy and sell student tickets at a fair market price.

View Events

Buff Market is a marketplace for students to buy or sell student tickets at market value. Students register with their CU identikey to ensure authenticity. Buyers and sellers can view listings, connect through the marketplace, and use a secure checkout process.

Key Features

  • Student Identification Login
  • Ability to post and bid on ticket offerings
  • 3rd Party Ticket Transfer
  • Secure Payment Integration
  • Market Price Orderbook
  • Share Listing Feature
